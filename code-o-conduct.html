### Code of Conduct The following rules and guidelines apply to anyone who attends edindies or GameDevEd or joins our Discord server. This is a living document. Like the community itself, it is open to changes. It is an attempt to lay down some basic principles that we can all agree upon, so that people know their rights and responsibilities. #### Together, we strive to: - Have a broad definition of what a game is, who a gamer or player is, and most of all who a developer or creator of games is. All sorts deserve to be treated equally. - Be friendly and welcoming to everyone, regardless of their race, ethnicity, culture, religion, national origin, colour, immigration status, social and economic class, education level, sexual orientation, gender identity and expression, age, size, family status, and mental and physical ability, among other things. - Be aware of social issues and how they affect our interpersonal relationships, and take action to correct for any biases we may have and injustices that others face. - Encourage those with less experience than ourselves with particular tools, rather than scaring them away. Everyone deserves to reach their full game dev potential! - Be empathetic when our experiences and perspectives differ. Aim to resolve disagreements and differing views constructively. - Take care with what we say and do, and make amends when we hurt others. - In short, dinnae be a dick. #### We will not: - Use violence or threats of violence to resolve conflict. - Use sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, ableist or otherwise discriminatory insults, jokes or other language. - Photograph or record anyone at an event without their consent. - Make inappropriate physical contact.
